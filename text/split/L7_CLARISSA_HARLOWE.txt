                        Twelve o' clock.
                  THIS moment the keys of every thing are taken from me. It was proposed to send for me down: But my papa said, he could not bear to look upon me.ÑStrange alteration in a few weeks! Shorey was the messenger. The tears stood in her eyes when she deliver'd her message.
                  You, my dear, are happy!ÑMay you always be so!ÑAnd then I can never be wholly miserable. Adieu, my beloved friend!
                     CL. HARLOWE.
