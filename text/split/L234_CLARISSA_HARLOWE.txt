A very few words, my ever-honour'd Mamma!
                        IF my sister wrote the inclosed by my father's direction, or yours, I must submit to the usage, with this <i>only</i> observation, That it is short of the personal treatment I have received from her. If it be of her own head:—Why then, Madam—But I <i>knew,</i> that when I was banish'd from your presence—Yet, till I know, if she has or has not authority for this usage, I will only write further, that I am
                           
                              <i>Your</i> very <i>unhappy Child,</i> CL. HARLOWE.
